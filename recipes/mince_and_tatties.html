Mince and Tatties

Traditional Scottish winter warmer

Straight from Granny's kitchen into your cold, empty tummy this dish of tender minced beef and creamy mashed potatoes will have you coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 1 stick of celery, diced
  • 500g beef mince (we used 12% fat mince)
  • 1.2kg potatoes
  • 4 tbsp plain flour
  • 450ml beef stock
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 50g butter
