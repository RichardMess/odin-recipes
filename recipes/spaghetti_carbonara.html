Spaghetti Carbonara

Classic egg, cheese and panchetta pasta dish from Rome

Only four main ingredients are needed to bring this simple, but delicious plate of spaghetti to life.

Ingredients

  • 100g panchetta
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 3 large eggs
  • 350g spaghetti
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and left whole
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • sea salt and freshly ground pepper
