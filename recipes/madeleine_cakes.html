Madeleine Cakes

Traditional, shell shaped sponge cake from France.

These small, shell shaped sponge cakes, from Northeast France, are quick and easy to make and go great with a cup of tea or coffee.

Ingredients

  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 100g/3.5oz caster sugar
  • 100g.3.5oz plain flour, extra for dusting
  • 1 lemon, juice and zest
  • 0.75 tsp baking powder
  • 100g/3.5oz butter, melted and cooled slightly, plus extra for greasing
